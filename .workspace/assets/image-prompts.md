## Mascot "ดอง" — Capybara

### Character Design Brief
- ชื่อ: **ดอง** (Dong)
- สัตว์: Capybara
- บุคลิก: calm, deadpan, observant — ไม่ยิ้มกว้าง ไม่ขมวดคิ้ว แค่ "เห็นทุกอย่าง"
- Accessory: **ไม่มี** (เอาดินสอออกแล้ว)
- Style: Flat vector chibi, LINE sticker aesthetic
- Palette: warm brown `#8B6343`, cream belly `#F5EFE6`, sage green accent `#7A9E7E`

```

Not smiling wide — just a neutral "I see everything" expression.

Physical design:
- Chibi capybara, large round head, stubby body
- Warm caramel brown (#8B6343), cream/beige belly
- NO accessories on the head or body — clean, simple look
- Round soft shapes, no sharp edges

Show on one sheet:
1. Front view — default neutral face
2. 3/4 view
3. 4 expressions:
   - neutral (default)
   - side-eye / suspicious (eyebrow slightly raised)
   - eyes-closed content smile (task complete)
   - rare wide-eyes surprised

Style: flat vector, clean bold outlines, warm color palette,
white background, LINE sticker / Sanrio-adjacent aesthetic
```
